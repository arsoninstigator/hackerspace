Hackerspace - An after-school girls-in-tech club





Hackerspace is an after-school club in New Delhi, India that caters towards middle-school / high-school-aged girls and nudges them in the direction of STEM/tech and coding. Since we are accepting young girls at any skill level (be it beginners to advanced) to become members and a part of the initiative, this is done by running coding workshops, building projects together, inviting mentors, asking questions and a whole lot of trial and error.


We’re affiliated with Hack Club and host a lot of events focussed around their programs (like high seas, boba drops, etc.). We’re always looking for donations, be it monetary or in-kind as a sponsorship deal. We would love for you to donate your time to sit down for an AMA session with those in the tech industry and give us an idea of what it’s like in India specifically.
